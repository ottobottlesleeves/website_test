About Otto Bottle Sleeves

Revolutionizing hydration with sustainable, stylish protection

Our Story

More than a water bottle. For too long, water bottles have been limited by the ordinary appearance and excessive cost of stickers for decoration. With full expression and maximum protection at half the price of stickers and bottle boots, Otto Bottle Sleeves are the perfect solution for demonstrating your personality on water bottles.

At Otto Bottle Sleeves, we believe that staying hydrated shouldn't come at the cost of our planet. Our journey began with a simple observation: millions of single-use water bottles were being discarded daily, contributing to environmental pollution.

We set out to create a solution that would not only protect your favorite reusable water bottle but also make a statement about sustainability. Our premium bottle sleeves are crafted from eco-friendly materials, designed to last, and styled to complement your lifestyle.

Every sleeve tells a story - whether it's your university pride, your love for the outdoors, or your commitment to making environmentally conscious choices. Otto Bottle Sleeves helps share common values to help find new friends. We're proud to partner with universities and organizations that share our vision for a greener future.
